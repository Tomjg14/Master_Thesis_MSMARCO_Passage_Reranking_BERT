passage:			Passage: Statute of Limitations Definition. Statute of limitations is a blanket term used to describe state statutes that specify the time limit that a plaintiff has to file a complaint or that the prosecutor has to file criminal charges. Thus, if this time has passed, the plaintiff or prosecutor is barred from initiating a case against the defendant.
